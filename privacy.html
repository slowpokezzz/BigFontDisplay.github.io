Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 7, 2026

Overview

BigFont Display is designed to turn your iPhone or iPad into a clear full-screen message display. The app does not require an account and does not collect personal data from you.

Data Collection

BigFont Display does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data with the developer.

Messages and App Settings

Messages, pages, display preferences, lock settings, and similar app settings are stored locally on your device. They are not sent to the developer.

Translation

BigFont Display may use Apple translation features available on your device. The developer does not receive the messages you translate.

In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not receive your full payment card details.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

  • BigFont Display does not include third-party advertising.
  • BigFont Display does not use third-party analytics.
  • BigFont Display does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.
