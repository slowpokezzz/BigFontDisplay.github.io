BigFont Display turns your iPhone or iPad into a simple full-screen message display for quiet rooms, noisy places, pickup signs, events, classrooms, travel, and accessibility moments.

Quick Start

1. Type

Open BigFont Display and double-tap the screen to edit your message.

2. Show

Tap once to show or hide controls, then hold up your iPhone or iPad.

3. Adjust

Change size, color, font, alignment, pages, translations, and lock controls from the menu.

Features

Message Display

Show one large message in a clean full-screen view.

Pages

Split longer messages into pages and move through them clearly.

Translation Unlock

Use the optional one-time purchase to display messages in multiple languages.

Lock Controls

Lock the controls before handing over your device or holding it up in public.

Font and Color

Choose the look that reads best for your message and setting.

iPhone and iPad

Use a pocket-sized sign on iPhone or a larger display on iPad.

FAQ

How do I edit the message?

Double-tap the display to edit. Tap once to show or hide controls.

Is Translation Unlock a subscription?

No. Translation Unlock is planned as a one-time in-app purchase.

What if I forget my app lock PIN?

The reset path is deleting and reinstalling the app. Offloading the app may preserve local data and may not reset the PIN.

Does BigFont Display require an account?

No. The app does not require sign-in or an account.

Privacy

Simple by design

BigFont Display does not require an account and is designed around local message display. Purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store.
